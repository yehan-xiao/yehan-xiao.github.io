description: Semi-Automatic Utility Classification System (SUCS) is a system that is designed to automate the discovery, mapping and documentation of key city infrastructure items through the analysis of commercially available, street-level imagery.<p>SUCS utilises deep-learning based approaches to identify these key infrastructure items, allowing for the automated discovery and mapping of these items. In doing so, the aim of SUCS is to allow city planners and infrastructure designers to quickly and accurately map a city's current infrastructure.</p><p>传统的城市公共设施定位方法是人为逐个勘测，虽定位精准，却需要大量的人力物力，且测量周期长。半自动城市公共设施分类系统则采用深度学习的方法，利用Faster RCNN算法训练出可以识别出多种城市公共设施的模型，进而系统通过一个地区的街景地图来快速估算各种公共设施的位置。</p><p><a href="http://v.youku.com/v_show/id_XMjk5NDU2OTI1Ng==.html?spm=a2hzp.8244740.0.0" target="blank">Demo Video</a>|<a href="https://github.com/yehan-xiao/SUCS" target="blank">Code Repository</a>|<a href="https://github.com/yehan-xiao/SUCS-wiki/blob/master/home.md" target="blank">Project Index</a></p>
